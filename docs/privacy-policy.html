Shadow Blaster — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 May 2026

This policy describes what data the Shadow Blaster iOS app collects, why it collects it, and how it is used. Plain English; no surprises.

What we collect

  • Anonymous subscription identifier. When you start a free trial or subscribe, our payment provider (RevenueCat, on top of Apple's StoreKit) generates a random anonymous user ID for your device. This is used to remember whether your subscription is active and to power "Restore Purchases."
  • Subscription status. Whether you are subscribed and when the subscription was purchased, renewed, or canceled.
  • Diagnostic data Apple may send. If you opt in to "Share with App Developers" in iOS Settings, Apple sends us anonymized crash logs and performance metrics. We use these to fix bugs.

What we do not collect

  • Your name, email, address, phone number, or Apple ID.
  • Your contacts, photos, location, or microphone.
  • Advertising IDs (IDFA / IDFV). The app contains no third-party SDKs other than RevenueCat.
  • Game telemetry. Your scores, ship build, mission progress, settings, Hall of Fame entries, and Codex are all stored only on your device, in iOS WebKit local storage. We never see them.

Where data goes

  • Apple — handles the actual subscription transaction. See apple.com/privacy.
  • RevenueCat — receives the anonymous subscription identifier and entitlement status only. Their privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy.
  • That's it. We do not run our own servers. We do not sell, rent, or trade any data.

Children

Shadow Blaster is rated 9+ and is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

Your rights

  • Cancel. Subscriptions can be canceled at any time in Settings → your name → Subscriptions on iOS.
  • Restore. Tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall.
  • Delete. Deleting the app deletes all on-device data. Anonymous subscription records held by RevenueCat can be removed on request — email us.
  • EU / UK / California residents — you have the right to access, rectify, erase, and port your data. Email us; we will respond within 30 days.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the "last updated" date above. For material changes we will additionally surface a notice in the app on next launch.
